Ablitteraphobia

Ablitteraphobia or strange letter syndrom is a fear disorder for the letter Q. Patients suffering from this phobia show mild to heavy anxiety from hearing, seeing and pronouncing the letter Q. They also severely avoid stuff associated with the letter Q.
