Types

There are different ways in which Ablitteraphobia shows itself. People can get affected by it at an early age, but it can also appear suddenly in other stages of life. Scientific research shows that there are three big models of how the phobia gets manifested. On this page you can read about these gradations of fear.

Light

The light form of Ablitteraphobia shows itself in a fear for the letter Q. This fear mostly develops at a young age. Triggers are problems with learning to read and dyslexia. For some, fear of the unknown can also trigger the letter phobia.

Moderate

People who suffer from a moderate form of the phobia also get triggered by the letter P. Because "p" is the mirror image of the letter "q" it can also disturb patients. The fear of the letter P is less intense than it's Q variant.

Heavy

In the heavy type of Ablitteraphobia the letter Q and the letter P are both equally overwhelming. The reactions to these letters can be quite extreme. This type of the phobia is often due to an event of trauma.
