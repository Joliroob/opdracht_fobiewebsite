Testimonials

Greta, 47 years old

When I was young me and my brothers liked playing Scrabble. I really enjoyed the cosiness of playing a boardgame in front of the fireplace. Everything goes fine except for that one time.... My (not so) innocent hand picked a Q from the letterbag. Just holding the piece in my hand made me shiver! I started screaming and breathing heavily. I had to stop the game and go to my room to recover from this terrible situation. I don't play Scrabble anymore.

Just holding the piece in my hand gives me the shivers!

Andrea, 24 years old

Healthy and vegan food is very popular nowadays. I enjoy organic meals, but there is one thing I can't cope with: quinoa. Sandwich and saladbars often serve quinoa. I totally dislike it. I can't stand that it's written with a Q. The word starts with a Q, so I'm immediately startled by it. Once, I tried to eat some, but I coudn't swallow it. Leave that damn quinoa trend behind, please! There are enough special foods to rave about.

There is one thing I can't cope with and it's quinoa!

Sometimes I get an overwhelming feeling from a place.

Miguel, 17 years old

I started noticing my fear of Q's when I go shopping with my mother or when I'm hanging out with friends in the city center. Sometimes I get an overwhelming feeling from a place. With time I recognized a pattern. It were all places with a Q in the brand name. We can't go tanking at a Q8 station when I'm inside the car. I can't shop surfing gear from the brand Quicksilver. Don't get me started about eating a burger and fries at Quick. Luckily, there aren't that much Q-brands.

Go upwards
