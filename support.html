What exactly?

Abli npo is working on the concept of a q-less label for products and services. This label would inform consumers that the product or service in question is free of the use of the letter Q.

To launch our label we are in need of an amount of money to initiate the label, make associations with other organizations and to introduce the label via readings and workshops at different schools and workgroups.
